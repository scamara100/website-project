Hero Section

Superman is a fictional superhero who first appeared in DC Comics publications in 1938 and has since become a global pop culture icon. He is known for his superhuman strength, speed, flight, and unwavering commitment to truth and justice.

Spider-Man is a superhero in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. Created by writer-editor Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko, he first appeared in the anthology comic book Amazing Fantasy #15 in the Silver Age of Comic Books.

Captain America is the superhero alter ego of Steve Rogers, a World War II-era hero who was given the Super Soldier serum to become a super-soldier. He is a symbol of American ideals, a powerful hero, and the leader of the Avengers.
